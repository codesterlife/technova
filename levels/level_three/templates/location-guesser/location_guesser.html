Task Guidelines

  • There is no time limit.
  • You are not allowed to search for the locations in Google maps search box or in any another searching facility.

  • You have to find all locations to complete the task.
  • Your task will only end after your handler clicks the "End Task" button
  • You are not allowed to click the "End Task" button yourself.
