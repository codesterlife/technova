Figure Out The Hidden Message

In the serene village of Evergreen Vale, Ouroboros lived a life of quiet reflection, captivated by the mysteries of the surrounding wilderness. A chance discovery—an aged parchment bearing cryptic symbols—ignited his curiosity and set him on an extraordinary journey. Seeking counsel from village elders, he learned of a legendary scroll said to unveil Blackwood Creek's secrets.

Driven by an insatiable thirst for knowledge, Ouroboros left behind his familiar world, guided by whispers of forgotten lore and the forest's ancient murmurs. On his journey to the unknown he carried with him, in his mind, a chant that gave him immense mental fortitude in the most daring of predicaments that he might come across on his journey, taught to him by his mother who was a beloved white witch of his land. She taught him this chant in a certain cryptic way so as to not be used by evil forces dormant in the realm. On his path into the forest he decides to decrypt this mantra to reveal its true essence.
